Welcome to my personal academic website! I am currently studying an MSc in Mathematics and Foundations of Computer Science at University of Oxford, with my mathematical interests remaining broad, though focussing on topics in combinatorics, especially those of a probabilistic and extremal nature.

During my undergraduate, I had the privilege of working under [Dr. Yue Ren](https://www.yueren.de/) and [Dr. Victoria Schleis](https://victoriaschleis.github.io/index.html) on an OSCAR package for computing the tropicalisation of zero-dimensional varieties of polynomial systems over local fields:
  * See [arXiv](https://arxiv.org/abs/2511.23298) for the pre-print.
  * You can also find the codebase for the implementation of the algorithms at [https://github.com/armanmarti-shahandeh/Zerodimensional-Tropicalisation/](https://github.com/armanmarti-shahandeh/Zerodimensional-Tropicalisation/), to be later appended to the tropical sub-library of OSCAR in the package $$\texttt{zero_dim_tropicalization.jl}$$.
